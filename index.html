About Us

Deload Logistics LLC was founded with one mission: to deliver freight with reliability, integrity, and efficiency. With a growing fleet of 45+ trucks and 50+ professional drivers, we serve clients across the continental U.S. Our headquarters in Cincinnati, OH allows us to operate centrally and provide responsive solutions to shippers nationwide.

Logistics is more than moving freight—it’s about trust, communication, and long-term partnerships. Our commitment to innovation and driver support sets us apart.
